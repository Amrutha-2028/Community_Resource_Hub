Come Unity

About Come Unity

ComeUnity was created to foster an environment where community members can connect with one another, support each other, and access the resources they need to thrive. This hub provides a way for people to meaningfully connect without barriers and helps strengthen neighborhood ties. It offers trusted information, coordinated support, and channels for community communication.

How this hub works

The hub collects and highlights resources across several categories so residents can quickly find help:

  • Food assistance
  • Housing support
  • Religious community assistance
  • Animal welfare
  • Other local services and programs

We partner with local organizations to provide services, support, and information. Examples include:

  • Nonprofit organizations
  • Religious institutions
  • Community groups
  • Local government agencies

    • These partners help us coordinate resources and deliver services to the community.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to bring programs, opportunities, and services together in one place so every person can find help, give help, and feel a sense of belonging. We also work to strengthen local connections and reduce barriers to access.

Who our community serves

We serve individuals, families, volunteers, and local organizations — anyone who wants to contribute to or benefit from a stronger, more connected community.

Our Vision

At ComeUnity Space, everyone belongs by sharing support, offering help, and finding help together in one place.
